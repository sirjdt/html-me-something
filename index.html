An Introduction to my Feline Friends

Prince

Prince is a Siamese cat I adopted from a shelter 15 years ago in Anchorage, Alaska. He is extremely clingy and talkative. Despite putting him on a diet for his obesity he always manages to steal my dog's food.

Nemo and Dory

I came into possession of these two Domestic Shorthairs within the same month. The one on the left is Nemo: he was a stray cat who hid under the shed outside my home. The one on the right is Dory: she was adopted to keep Nemo company since he and Prince didn't get along. Nemo is very chill while Dory is very needy and eats the other cats' food.

Skipper

Skipper is a stray black cat who visits a few times a week. He's very elusive but manages to eat all the food I leave out for him. Hopefully he doesn't give me bad luck in exchange for my generosity.
